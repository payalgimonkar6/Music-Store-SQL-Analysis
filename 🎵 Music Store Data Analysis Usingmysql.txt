# 🎵 Music Store Data Analysis Using PostgreSQL

## 📖 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a Music Store database using PostgreSQL to answer business-related questions and generate meaningful insights from customer purchases, music genres, artists, and sales data.

The project demonstrates SQL skills ranging from basic queries to advanced analytical queries involving joins, subqueries, aggregations, and Common Table Expressions (CTEs).

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify top customers and revenue-generating countries.
* Discover the most popular music genres.
* Find top-performing artists.
* Generate business insights using SQL.

## 📊 Business Questions Solved

### Easy Level

1. Who is the senior most employee based on job title?
2. Which countries have the most invoices?
3. What are the top 3 invoice totals?
4. Which city generates the highest revenue?
5. Who is the best customer?

### Moderate Level

1. Find all Rock music listeners.
2. Identify the top 10 Rock artists.
3. Find songs longer than the average song length.

### Advanced Level

1. Calculate the amount spent by each customer on artists.
2. Determine the most popular music genre for each country.
3. Find the highest-spending customer in each country.
